Bio

Haixi Li is a Senior Economist at Freddie Mac. His research interest includes time series econometrics, economic and financial forecasting and Bayesian analysis. He attained his Ph.D in Economics at University of Wisconsin-Madison. He has published in the International Journal of Forecasting.
